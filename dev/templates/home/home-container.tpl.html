Welcome To MetaTruth!

This app is designed to help answer the most complex and controversial questions that our society faces

MetaTruth is a three tier system consisting of:
  1. Questions
  2. Main Points
  3. Support Points

Questions

On MetaTruth, everything is rooted back to main questions. Questions follow three main rule. They:

  1. Should be Yes or No questions only
  2. Should focus on one thing and not a comparison
    • GOOD: Should we go to mars?
    • BAD: What is better, linux server or windows server?
  3. Should be complex questions that will require many points to answer.
    • BAD: Do humans need oxygen to live?

      • There are plenty of other resources to get or post answers to questions like these. Such as:

      • Quora
      • Yahoo Answers
      • Google
